RIXOS

This luxurious 5-star hotel is located in Almaty city centre, 1 km from St. Nicholas Cathedral. Rixos Almaty Hotel offers modern rooms with free Wi-Fi and a spa area with a swimming pool. All air-conditioned rooms at Rixos Almaty come with a work desk and a balcony with great views. The lobby bar and lounge are open 24/7. There is also a Brasserie restaurant. The 2,200 m2 spa includes a gym, sauna, Turkish bath, indoor garden and 7 massage rooms.
